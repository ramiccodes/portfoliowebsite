About Ramic Laguitan

Frontend Development Intern

Born and raised in Manila, Philippines and immigrated to Vancouver, BC back in 2017.

My earliest memories are being drawn towards creative endeavors like reading, writing, movies, and design. They would interest me enough that they would turn into my hobbies, which I practiced and taught myself skills around.

Due to this, I am familiar with how to edit videos and photos, how to write and structure stories intuitively for both myself and the reader, and how to edit videos in a way that follows a planned storyline. All this while adding humor and personality to them. I put a little bit of myself in every creative project that I make.

Programming always fascinated me with the amount of things you could do with it. I initially got into programming due to my desire of automating some of my menial tasks. The first language that I learned was Python, though I was disappointed that it didn’t show an output like I was expecting. I later learned that it is because Python, like many programming language is normally used for the back-end.

I learned that web development is the ideal pathway for me as I can implement my creativity with programming and build stuff that I enjoy and something that would help someone discover their love of the arts like I did as a child.
