The 'X's And The 'O's

Tic Tac Toe Browser Game

Made with the similar design of this portfolio website in mind

I created a 3x3 grid to simulate the Tic Tac Toe gameboard and written a loop that selects each of the squares, in order to put down the player's turn inside. I gave the AI opponent the same moves but I set a timeoiut in order to simulate the AI thinking about its next move.

A selection screen for the player, whether they like to play as 'X' or 'O' is also included and upon clicking either of the buttons, the selection screen will fade out and the gameboard will fade in

I have also made the winning logic of the game, wherein if 3 of the same symbols match, the player or the AI will be declared the winner by the gameboard fading out and the victory screen fading in complete with a 'Replay' button to bring back the selection screen on.

You can assert humanity's dominance over the machine by clicking this link.
