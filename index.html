Latest Project

This Website!

Modeled after what I call the "The Miami Nightclub" aesthetic.

For its design, I utilized a shade of pink named #ff69b4 for text emphasis, a shade of black named #171321 for the background color of the "Projects" and "About" section, a shade of blue named #2b3871 for the body background color, and a shade of purple named #4b0d49 for the "Contact me" section.

I used the fonts, "Newreader" for the header texts and "Tiro Gurmukhi" for paragraph texts. I've also added links throughout in the form of spans and icons that change styles when you hover over them. I've also created a navbar that redirect you to a section of the page itself or links to another website when clicked.
